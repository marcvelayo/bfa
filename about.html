MARC VELAYO

ARTIST STATEMENT:

Hello, my name is Marc Velayo. I am an aspiring artist who ventures in different kinds of art medium-- both traditional and digital. Through creating art, I’ve
learned how to express my emotions-- my dreams, fears and thoughts. It has instilled courage and confidence in me to fearlessly depict my true self.

My work represents who I was in the past, what I feel in the present and who I want to be in the future. I am a proud immigrant-- born and raised in Antipolo,
Philippines. I was first introduced to using computers when I was 10. I remember playing around our Dell computer-- opening programs such as Paint and Adobe
Photoshop Elements. Who knew I’d be using these programs now to further my passion and career in art? I was just a kid playing and messing around, fascinated
by the vivid colors and simple strokes I was creating.

My work depicts the emotions I’ve felt and feel, and the vivid memories I’ve experienced and currently experiencing. I use my recollections as a strength and guide
in telling my story, to add a personal touch in every medium that I use. I am highly interested in innovation-- especially in the field of Technology. Being able
to take classes under Digital Media has opened my eyes to all the amazing possibilities of what technology and art can do. Technology and art together can create
powerful, highly engaging pieces and that’s what I want to pursue. My ambition in being a part of the BFA program is to learn more, create more and be heavily
immersed in the world of technology based art and concepts. I want to be a part of an amazing and talented community, where artists thrive in a space powered by
their knowledge in intersecting art and technology for expression.

I passionately want to use my voice and story to create pieces that can impact and influence our society. As a student, I’ve learned various programs to help me
further my knowledge in digital media such as the Adobe Creative Softwares, 3D Modeling software like Blender, languages such as HTML, CSS etc. I want to further
my journey as a Digital Media student, and gain more important lessons that I can use in my career and that can guide me in life.
